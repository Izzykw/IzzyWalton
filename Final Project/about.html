Welcome to My Empire

My name is Isabella Walton, and I'm a Young Adult Dystopian author!

I am a student at BYU, majoring in Finance, with a minor in Violin Performance; oh, and I'm also an author.

What inspired me to write "The Heiress?

This story was inspired from all the stories I made as a kid, playing pretend in the backyard. Everyone knows that drama goes down when five-year-olds play pretend.

Daydreamer, Night Thinker

I sit in class bored a lot. When I'm bored, my mind skips off to the brilliant worlds of my stories, painting vivid scenes and making new characters.
