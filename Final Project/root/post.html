Taking on BYU One Day at a Time

"Life moves pretty fast. If you don't stop and look around once in a while, you could miss it." -Ferris Bueller

Posted by Isabella Walton on December 18, 2024

Fact is, God is the reason in everything. I can't do anything I do without God.

God has given us everything and given us grace through the atonement of his son. All I can do to give back is give him everything. My heart, might, mind and strength.

What that looks like in daily life for me is doing my best. Offering all I have in a day to give back to him. I work hard in school, I save up financially for my future, I celebrate my talents and gifts, I spend time with friends, and I give back.

I'll be leaving on a mission for 18 months in feburary. I can't work or attend school during this time. I can't do a lot of recreational activities I usually do. It is a wholehearted religious dedication.

Why on Earth would someone like me serve a mission? To give back.

Tithing on our Life

I'm 19 years old, so serving a mission is technically 10 percent of my life. It is 19 months of the 19 years I have lived.

I want to be the best version of myself I can be, and that means serving a mission.

Financial Success

My dad started a custodial stock portfolio for me when I was 12 years old. Since then the account has grown 50 percent, and is helping me to graduate college without debt.

Defining Finance

I took over the account when I turned 18, and I proudly still invest 80% of every paycheck into the market.

Work Hard Now, Reap the Rewards Later.

You reap what you sow. I'm investing in my whole life

Take 1 percent of your day to invest in your future. That's 24 minutes. What will you do?
